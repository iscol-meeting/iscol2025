ISCOL 2025

The Israel Seminar on Computational Linguistics

Location: Wohl Conventions Center, Bar-Ilan University

Date: December 18th, 2025

Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Computational Linguistics are active research and development fields in Israel today, both in academia and industry. ISCOL is a venue that was established in 1995 and has been held continuously every year (almost). It serves as a venue for exchanging ideas, reporting on work in progress and established results, forming collaborations, and advancing connections between academia and industry. ISCOL is also a friendly stage for students making their first appearance in this community.

Registration will open shortly after the submission deadline

Follow us: @iscol_meeting

Call for Papers

  • Submission deadline: November 13th, 2025 (23:59 AoE)
  • Notification: November 20th, 2025 (23:59 AoE)
  • Submission site: OpenReview

The 2025 Israeli Seminar on Computational Linguistics (ISCOL 2025) invites presentations of recent work in all areas of natural language processing, computational linguistics and closely related fields. We accept work underway, provided that it represents recent and original work of interest to our audience, as well as work that has been submitted or published at other venues. Submitted papers do not need to be anonymous.

Submission format: Please submit your work as a PDF file. Use the *ACL format for extended abstracts, or the format required by the conference/journal for full papers.

We accept two types of submissions:

  1. Extended abstracts (up to 2 pages, not including references).
  2. Full papers that were submitted to other related venues. Please indicate at the end of the abstract field in the submission form whether the paper was accepted (and in this case, to which venue), or is currently under review.

FAQ

What is ISCOL?

ISCOL is the Israel Seminar on Computational Linguistics, a community venue for NLP and CL research.

Where is ISCOL 2025 taking place?

ISCOL 2025 will be held at the Wohl Conventions Center, Building 1401, Bar-Ilan University.
Note that the building is not inside the main campus, so you do not have to enter the campus itself.

Address: Wohl Conventions Center, Max ve-Anna Webb St, Ramat Gan, 5211501

Is ISCOL archival?

No. Works presented at ISCOL will not be considered as archived.

How does the review process work?

Submissions are lightly reviewed by the organizing committee to ensure relevance and quality. The process is not anonymous. The goal is to maintain a friendly and inclusive environment for sharing research.

Can I submit a paper that was published or accepted to be published in another venue?

Yes. Preferably if it was accepted (to be) published during 2025 and was not presented in previous ISCOL events.

Can I submit a full paper instead of an extended abstract?

Yes. Please indicate whether the paper was accepted (and venue), or is currently under review. A mature work (in progress) that has not yet been submitted to any venue is also fine.

What are the submission file format and style?

You should submit a PDF file, using the *ACL format if it's an extended abstract, or the format of the conference/journal for full papers.

Will I need to print my poster?

Yes. You will have to print your own posters, hang it in the break before your poster session and take it down during the break following your poster session.

What are the dimensions for the posters?

The poster stands are vertical, and their dimensions are 100 cm * 200 cm, so A0 (84.1 cm x 118.9 cm) is recommended.

What is the event language?

English. All presentations will be in English.

Where can I find information about previous ISCOL events?

ISCOL has been running since 1995. You can find information about previous iterations:

  • ISCOL 2024: University of Haifa
  • ISCOL 2022: Tel Aviv University
  • ISCOL 2021: Ben Gurion University
  • ISCOL 2020: Online
